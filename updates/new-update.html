CANADIAN SPEED SKATING SPRINT SPECIALIST AIMING FOR MILANO CORTINA 2026

Welcome to my first post and to my new website

by Christopher Fiola
~1 min read May 7, 2022

So I have decided to create a website about myself. Hopefully this will help promote me and eventually gather more attention into what I’m working towards.

The ultimate goal is Milan Cortina 2026 and we will see after…

Site’s Content

1. Homepage:

This is where you will find general information about me. You will find an agenda with upcoming events that I will participate in and I will also try to incorporate my latest results using speedskatingresults.com API.

2. What’s up:

This is where you will find the latest updates about me. I will include quotes on my recent results with a picture from the event. The updates will be shown in a chronogical order from latest to oldest in a grid view.

3. Hall Of Fame:

This is where you will find my best results and notable moments. I will divide this by skating seasons.

4. Work In Progress:

This is where you will find what projects I am currently working on. I will update this using posts and estimated finish time.
